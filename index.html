How it works

Three steps to your next content idea

No setup, no account, no data sent to any server we control. Your keys, your data.

1

Navigate to a page

Open any YouTube video or Reddit thread you want to research. HarvestIQ detects it automatically.

2

Click Analyze

Hit the button in the side panel. Up to 100 comments are fetched and sent to your AI provider.

3

Get insights

In seconds you have questions, pain points, content angles, and key phrases — ready to use or export.
